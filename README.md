A browser-based photobooth designed with a classic silver-body camera aesthetic. This app transforms your webcam into a vintage digital camera experience, capturing three sequential shots and automatically stitching them into a vertical film strip with an authentic early-2000s digital aesthetic.

## üåü Key Features

* **Silver Body UI:** A custom interface designed to mimic the metallic look of early-2000s digital cameras.
* **Sequential Capture:** A precision-timed session that takes 3 separate photos in a row.
* **Live Countdown:** A large, centered **3.. 2.. 1..** timer to help you time your poses perfectly.
* **Vertical Film Strip:** Automatically generates a high-resolution "carnival-style" photo strip.
* **Vintage Styling:** Finished with warm color processing and a classic orange digital date stamp.

## üöÄ How to Use

1.  **Power On:** Press the **PWR** button to initialize the camera system.
2.  **Label:** Type a custom caption in the input box to label your memories.
3.  **Capture:** Click the large silver **SHUTTER** button to start the session.
4.  **Pose:** Follow the on-screen countdown for each of the three snaps.
5.  **Review:** Scroll through the "Development Lab" on the right to see your full vertical strip.
6.  **Export:** Click **EXPORT TO DISK** to save your photo as a PNG.
